# Bang mo ta chuc nang trang web

Tai lieu nay tom tat cac chuc nang chinh cua FLIC AI Operations Dashboard theo nhom nguoi dung va man hinh trong ung dung.

| STT | Nhom chuc nang | Man hinh / thanh phan | Vai tro su dung | Mo ta ngan |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Dang nhap va phan quyen | Login | Admin, Staff | Cho phep dang nhap bang tai khoan demo va dieu huong vao khong gian lam viec tuong ung voi vai tro. |
| 2 | Dieu huong he thong | Sidebar | Admin, Staff | Hien thi menu theo vai tro, giup nguoi dung chuyen nhanh giua cac man hinh nghiep vu. |
| 3 | Thanh tieu de | Header | Admin, Staff | Hien thi ten man hinh hien tai, thong bao, avatar va cac thao tac tai khoan nhu thong tin ca nhan, cai dat, tro giup, dang xuat. |
| 4 | Tong quan van hanh | Tong quan | Admin | Hien thi KPI tong hop ve hoi thoai, tin nhan, trang thai xu ly va cau hoi AI that bai. |
| 5 | Phan tich kenh | Kenh | Admin | Theo doi hieu qua tung kenh Zalo Business, Facebook, Zalo OA va Chat Widget bang bang so lieu, bieu do va heatmap. |
| 6 | Quan ly hoi thoai | Hoi thoai | Admin, Staff | Hien thi danh sach hoi thoai, noi dung trao doi, trang thai xu ly va cac hanh dong nghiep vu lien quan den phan hoi khach hang. |
| 7 | Phan tich AI | Phan tich AI | Admin | Tong hop loi AI, xu huong cau tra loi that bai, muc do anh huong va cac de xuat can xu ly. |
| 8 | Phan tich tu khoa | Keywords | Admin | Nhom va thong ke cac chu de quan tam nhu TOEIC, VSTEP, Tin hoc / MOS / IC3 va Chuan dau ra / Chung chi. |
| 9 | Phan tich cam xuc | Cam xuc | Admin | Theo doi ty le tich cuc, trung lap, tieu cuc va cac dau hieu bat thuong trong hoi thoai khach hang. |
| 10 | Tao bieu do | Bieu do | Admin | Cho phep chon truong du lieu, cau hinh truc, gia tri, bo loc va xem truoc bieu do truoc khi luu vao dashboard. |
| 11 | Quan ly thu vien phan hoi | Thu vien phan hoi | Admin, Staff | Luu tru cau hoi - cau tra loi dung, theo doi trang thai duyet va ho tro cap nhat tri thuc chatbot. |
| 12 | Quan ly FAQ | FAQ | Staff | Cho phep xem chi tiet FAQ, de xuat chinh sua noi dung va tham khao cau tra loi AI theo tung chu de. |
| 13 | Hieu suat ca nhan | Hieu suat | Staff | Hien thi so lieu xu ly hoi thoai, toc do phan hoi, muc do hai long va xu huong hieu suat cua nhan vien. |
| 14 | Ho so ca nhan | Ho so / Thong tin ca nhan | Admin, Staff | Quan ly thong tin nguoi dung, cau hinh thong bao, kenh phu trach va tuy chon hien thi hoi thoai. |
| 15 | Cai dat he thong | Cai dat | Admin | Quan ly nguon du lieu, cau hinh kenh, thong bao va nguoi dung - phan quyen trong he thong. |
| 16 | Quan ly nguoi dung | Nguoi dung & phan quyen | Admin | Quan ly danh sach nhan su, vai tro, kenh phu trach, quyen thao tac va trang thai tai khoan. |
| 17 | Thong bao | Notification dropdown | Admin, Staff | Hien thi cac viec can chu y theo vai tro va cho phep dieu huong nhanh den man hinh lien quan. |
| 18 | Tro ly AI noi bo | AI Chat Widget | Admin, Staff | Ho tro hoi nhanh ve so lieu dashboard, canh bao va goi y hanh dong dua tren du lieu demo. |

## Ghi chu

- Admin / Manager tap trung vao tong quan he thong, phan tich du lieu, quan ly tri thuc chatbot va cau hinh van hanh.
- Staff tap trung vao hoi thoai duoc giao, FAQ, thu vien phan hoi, hieu suat ca nhan va ho so lam viec.
- Du lieu hien tai la du lieu demo phuc vu trinh bay va kiem thu giao dien.
